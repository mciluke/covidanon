Flatten the Curve Anonymously

If you recently tested positive for COVID-19, anyone who you've had recent exposure to could be at risk and should consider getting tested.1
CovidAnon.org offers a safe, anonymous, and completly free way of disclosing positive test results to those who you've recently been in contact with, via text message.

How it works

Received a text from this site?

If you received a text message from this site, someone who has your phone number wants to alert you that you may have been exposed to COVID-19. Please refer to CDC, WHO, and/or your local health authority for further guidance.

1. Tested Positive

Someone tested positive for COVID-19, either through the Antigen test, Polymerise Chain Reaction (PCR) test, or Antibody test.

2. Anonymous Disclosure

They visited CovidAnon.org and entered your phone number to anonymously disclose their positive test results.

3. Flatten the Curve

Together, you fight the spread of coronavirus by raising awareness and following the latest guidelines from the health authorities.
